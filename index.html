Progressive Enhancement Writing Assignment

Definition: Progressive Enhancement refers to the mindset of focusing web development on content (html) first and foremost, then styling content(css), and finally interactive functionality (js).

PURPOSE: As various browsers continue to be created with varying support and capabilities, Enhancement Writing allows developers -and in-turn gives users- a universal format and methodology to make content available on any platform; all without neglecting support to older browser and providing a standard for future support moving forward.
